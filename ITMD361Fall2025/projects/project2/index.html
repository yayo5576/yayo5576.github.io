For my future career, I would want to become a IT Technican.

IT technicians mainly do the responsibilities of installing, fixing, and troubleshooting both software and hardware in a computer system. They mainly provide many services to support, maintain, and repair the hardware and software of a company's computer systems.

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for computer user support specialists in May 2024, was $60,340. The employment overall is predicted to decrease by 3 percent from 2024 to 2034. Despite this, around 50,500 openings for the role are expected annually for the next decade. To become a IT Technician or Computer Support Specialist, having relevant IT technican certifcations such as CompTIA A+ and Network+, are more likely to increase the chances of getting employed.
